Beatbox Tool

If you are looking for real tool for beatboxers, this place is for you. Here you can find new sequences of beatbox noises. Just press to show folders with noises to choose. Open folders, choose noises you need, you'll see them on the left bar - to delete noises just click any noise here or click to remove all chosen noises. Press again to and enjoy your permutations! Click any permutation to play it with noises I recorded. Tool generates permutations automatically

To come back here, press button.

Title: "Project and implementation of software to generate sequences of sounds for music using".
